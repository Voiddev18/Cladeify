## 🧬 What is Cladeify?
Cladeify is an interactive puzzle where players identify a mystery animal by narrowing down its taxonomic classification. Each day presents a new challenge to test your knowledge of the tree of life.

## 🕹️ Features
- **Daily Challenge**: A new puzzle every 24 hours.
- **Interactive Tree**: Navigate an unfolding taxonomic visualization based on your guesses.
- **Scientific Depth**: Discover clades and biodiversity through an academic-style interface.
