📖 Field Guide

Master the art of phylogenetic deduction

🎯 The Objective

Find the Mystery Animal in as few guesses as possible. Each daily puzzle features a unique species from the tree of life.

🧬 Taxonomy Logic

Every guess reveals the Most Recent Common Ancestor (MRCA)—the point where your guess and the answer branch off.

🟥 Red: Distant relative. 🟨 Yellow: Same Class or Order. 🟩 Green: Same Family or Genus. 🟦 Blue: Target Species found!

🌳 The Living Tree

The tree visualization isn't just for show! Click on any node to open its Wikipedia Knowledge Panel—revealing common species and biological history.
