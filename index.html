📖 How to Play

Cladeify is a daily phylogenetic guessing game.

  • Guess any animal to start.
  • The tree will reveal the most recent common ancestor (clade) between your guess and the mystery animal.
  • Use the taxonomic clues and proximity colors to narrow down your next guesses!

Color Guide:

  • 🟥 Red: Very distant.
  • 🟨 Yellow: Getting closer (same class or order).
  • 🟩 Green: Very close (same family or genus).
  • 🟦 Cyan: Direct hit!
