# Cladeify Evolution Concepts

This document outlines potential features, gamification mechanics, and event ideas to increase player engagement and retention.

## 1. Progression & Rewards
*   **Taxon Tokens (Currency):** Earned by completing games. Bonuses for fewer guesses.
*   **Scientific Ranking System:** Players progress from "Amateur Observer" to "Master Taxonomist" based on total wins or win rate.
*   **Leveling System:** Gain XP for every guess made and game won. Higher levels unlock new UI themes or icons.
*   **Milestone Rewards:** Special badges for reaching 10, 50, 100, and 365 days of play.

## 2. Event Ideas
*   **Extinction Weekends (Double Points):** Every Saturday and Sunday, all point earnings and XP are doubled.
*   **"Wildcard Wednesday":** Instead of a random animal, the answer is picked from a specific, ultra-rare group (e.g., deep-sea creatures or extinct species like the Dodo).
*   **Evolution Rush:** A timed mode where players try to identify 3 animals in a row as fast as possible.
*   **Global Collaboration Events:** If the community collectively correctly identifies 100,000 animals in a month, everyone unlocks a "Golden Tree" skin.

## 3. Social & Competition
*   **Research Labs (Clans/Teams):** Join a group of friends. The average streak of the lab is ranked on a secondary leaderboard.
*   **Head-to-Head Duels:** Challenge a friend to the same puzzle; whoever finds the answer in fewer guesses or less time wins "Prestige."
*   **Regional Leaderboards:** Filter the leaderboard by country or city to see the top biologists in your area.

## 4. Customization (Tree Skins)
Players can spend Taxon Tokens or reach level milestones to unlock:
*   **Nature Theme:** Tree lines look like actual branches with leaves; nodes are wood-textured.
*   **Cyber-Grid:** Neon lines, glowing nodes, and a matrix-like background.
*   **Parchment:** Lines look like hand-drawn ink on old, weathered yellow paper (Historical Journal style).
*   **Deep Sea:** Dark blue glows with bioluminescent connections.

## 5. Collection & Bestiary
*   **The Specimen Room:** Every animal you correctly identify is added to your personal "Specimen Room" (collection gallery).
*   **Trivia Cards:** Unlocking a specimen provides a collectible card with more fun facts than the standard Wikipedia snippet.
*   **Rarity Tiers:** Some animals are "Common" while others (like the Platypus) are "Legendary" specimens to collect.

## 6. Streak Mechanics
*   **Streak Protection (Save-a-Streak):** If you miss a day, you can spend a large amount of tokens or watch a "sponsored research" clip to keep your streak alive.
*   **Streak Multiplier:** Having a 7+ day streak adds a 1.2x multiplier to all points earned. A 30+ day streak adds 1.5x.
*   **Dynamic Fire:** The "fire" icon on the leaderboard gets bigger or changes color (Blue Fire) as the streak grows.
